Ovi Shekh (born May 14, 2004) is a Bangladeshi serial AI founder, entrepreneur, and technologist. He is the founder of Arklab AI, a venture studio focused on building agentic AI solutions, and was previously the founder of Wisdomic AI, which was acquired in 2023. Known for his contributions to the AI ecosystem in Bangladesh, he is a recipient of the BIG (Bangladeshi Innovation Grant).
